Welcome to NewPass

NewPass is an open-source password manager designed to keep your passwords secure and easily accessible. Explore our features and join our community on GitHub.

About NewPass

NewPass is developed with the goal of providing a simple and secure way to manage your passwords. It is fully open source, allowing you to inspect the code and contribute to its development.

Features

  • Secure storage of passwords with double AES encryption
  • Strong Password Generation
  • Open source and community-driven
  • Regular updates and security patches
  • User-Friendly Interface
